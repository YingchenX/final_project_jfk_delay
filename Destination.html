Could Destination Airport Affect Delay and Cancellation?


There are 66 and 65 destination airports in the delay and cancellation datasets, respectively. For the efficiency purpose in statistical analysis, we do not include destination airport as a predictor in our models. However, it is still an interesting factor to explore.


Overall Trend

First we would like to check if delay and cancellation counts differ in different destination airports.



Flights from JFK to LAX have the highest delay occurrences with a number of 2293.



Flights from JFK to SFO have the highest cancellation occurrences with a number of 76.
